A protocol for stablecoins

And for the apps that use them

Eco is designed to be the default protocol for apps that use stablecoins or need stablecoin liquidity. Eco provides a frictionless stablecoin experience that accelerates user onboarding and streamlines money movement onchain.

A full stack upgrade for transacting onchain
